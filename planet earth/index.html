This is the world we live in.

Earth, our home, is the third planet from the sun. It is the only planet known to have an atmosphere containing free oxygen, oceans of liquid water on its surface, and, of course, life. About the Earth

  • Diameter: 8,000 miles (13,000 kilometers)
  • Length of solar day: 23.934 hours
  • Equatorial inclination to orbit: 23.4393 degrees
  • Mass: 6.6 sextillion tons (5.9722 x 1024 kilograms)

Earth's Facts

  1. Earth was formed at roughly the same time as the sun and other planets some 4.6 billion years ago
  2. Earth's atmosphere is roughly 78 percent nitrogen, 21 percent oxygen and other gases.
  3. Earth's core is about 4,400 miles (7,100 km) wide, slightly larger than half the Earth's diameter and roughly the size of Mars.
  4. Earth's moon is 2,159 miles (3,474 km) wide, about one-fourth of Earth's diameter.
